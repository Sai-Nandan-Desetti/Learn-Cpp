◆ fractionRange

constexpr std::int8_t fractionRange { 100 }
constexpr

The range of the fractional component of the fixed-point number is given by (-fractionRange,fractionRange)

  • For a 2-decimal fixed-point number, it's 10sup{2};
    • For an n-decimal fixed-point number, it's 10sup{n}.
  • If tomorrow, you decide that you need a 3-decimal fixed-point numbers instead of 2, just change fractionRange here (to 10sup{3}).
